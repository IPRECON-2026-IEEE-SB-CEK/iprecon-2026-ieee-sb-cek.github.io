Housing Support

Accommodation assistance for international delegates attending IPRECON 2026

Exclusive Support for International Delegates

This housing support program is exclusively available for delegates from outside India, Nepal, and Bhutan, providing comfortable accommodation to ensure your focus remains on the conference and networking opportunities.

Important Notice

This support is exclusively for delegates from outside India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

Delegates from the USA, Canada, and Latin & Central America may be eligible for an additional complimentary night stay due to extended travel durations. Please contact the organizers for special assistance.

Accommodation Details

  • Accommodation will be provided in shared rooms (same gender) in a 4 or 5-star facility
  • Commutation to the venue will be arranged for all hosted participants
  • Accommodation will be arranged from September 3rd (night) to September 7th (morning)
  • All meals included during the stay period
  • 24/7 support and assistance available

Application Process

Follow these simple steps when the application period opens

1

Paper Acceptance

Wait for your paper to be accepted at IPRECON 2026. Housing support is only available for accepted papers.

2

Complete Registration

Complete your full conference registration and verify your IEEE membership status.

3

Submit Application

Submit your housing support application with all required documentation when applications open.

4

Receive Confirmation

Applications reviewed on first-come, first-served basis. You'll receive confirmation about your status.

Eligibility Criteria

Review the requirements below to determine your eligibility for housing support

1
The applicant must have a paper accepted at IPRECON 2026 and must complete full conference registration
2
The applicant must be an active IEEE Student or Graduate Student Member (GSM)
3
Priority will be given to applicants with valid IEEE PES and/or IAS Memberships
4
Applicants who do not wish to share accommodation may opt for reimbursement of 50% of the hotel cost

Important Information

Support is limited and will be granted on a first-come, first-served basis, subject to fund availability.

Reimbursements can be claimed after reaching the venue and completing the registration process.

Extended Travel Support

Delegates from the USA, Canada, and Latin & Central America may be eligible for an additional complimentary night stay due to extended travel durations. Please contact the organizers for special assistance and to discuss your specific travel requirements.
