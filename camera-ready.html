Camera-Ready Submission Instructions

Please follow the instructions below for submitting your final camera-ready paper.

Deadline for submission: June 30, 2026

Submission Process Overview

The camera-ready submission process consists of 5 essential steps that must be completed in order. Please follow each step carefully to ensure your paper is successfully included in the conference proceedings and IEEE Xplore® Digital Library.

1

Final Camera-Ready Paper Preparation

  • Follow the IEEE two-column A4-size standard conference format for your final manuscript. Refer to the IEEE Templates page for more details: https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html
  • Revised Version: Revise your paper based on reviewers' comments and suggestions (if any). Reviewers' comments are available in the Microsoft paper management system: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/IPRECON2026
  • The maximum page limit is six pages, including references. No author biography is allowed at the end. Additional pages (up to eight) can be included with an extra payment of Rs. 1000 ($25) per page.
  • The similarity index should be below 30%. Higher similarity scores are not acceptable. The maximum file size allowed is 3 MB, and the paper must be submitted in PDF format (.pdf) without encryption or password protection.
  • Ensure that all reviewer and meta-reviewer comments have been addressed. Responses to these comments should be provided in the "post author feedback" section in CMT. Additionally, upload a separate PDF file highlighting the changes as the author's feedback file.
Access CMT System IEEE Templates
2

PDF eXpress Check

Once revisions are complete, check your paper using PDF eXpress with Conference ID 68617.

  • Go to https://ieee-pdf-express.org
  • If you don't have an account, select "New Users – Click Here" and provide the required details (email and password).
  • For guidance, visit: https://ieee-pdf-express.org/External/UsingIEEEPDFExpress
Access PDF eXpress User Guide
3

Paper Submission

  • Upload the final version of your paper after passing the PDF eXpress check in the author console on CMT.
  • Rename the PDF eXpress-checked file as PIDxx (where xx is your paper ID in CMT).
  • Important: The title and author list in the original submission cannot be changed in the final paper.
File Naming Convention

Rename your PDF eXpress-validated file to: PIDxx.pdf

Where xx is your paper ID number from CMT

Examples:
Paper ID 23 → PID23.pdf
Paper ID 156 → PID156.pdf
Paper ID 7 → PID7.pdf
Submit to CMT
4

Submission of the Copyright Form

After uploading your final camera-ready paper:

  • Click on "Submit IEEE Copyright Form" in CMT.
  • You will be redirected to the IEEE eCF website to complete the copyright form and agreement.
  • Download the completed copyright form (a copy will also be emailed to the corresponding author).
  • Upload the signed copyright form in CMT under the "drop files here" menu.

Only the corresponding author can submit the copyright form for all authors.

5

Conference Registration

Conference registration is mandatory for the paper to be included in the conference proceedings. The registration portal will be available soon. Check the website for updates: https://iprecon.org/registration.html

Register for Conference

No-Show Policy

Each accepted paper in the final program must have at least one author present at the conference. Non-presented papers will not be submitted to IEEE Xplore®, as per IEEE's no-show policy.
