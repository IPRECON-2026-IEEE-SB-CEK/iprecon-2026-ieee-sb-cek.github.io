About

2022 IEEE International Power and Renewable Energy Conference (IPRECON) is the 3rd edition of India's first IEEE IAS Club of 6 Technical Paper Conference organized by IEEE Student Branch College of Engineering Karunagappally (IEEE SB CEK), technically sponsored by IEEE IAS, IEEE PES and IEEE PELS.

IPRECON provides a platform for students and professionals from all around the world to share their findings in the fields of Power, Renewable Energy and Computing Technologies. With the theme “Engineering the sustainable world with Renewable Energy Resources through ingenious computing technologies”, the conference will be held on the 16th , 17th and 18th December 2022 in College of Engineering Karunagappally, Kerala, India.
