Student Travel Support

Financial assistance for travel expenses for student authors from India, Nepal, and Bhutan attending IPRECON 2026

Regional Student Travel Support Available

Travel Support is available only for Student Authors from India, Nepal, and Bhutan. Delegates from other countries are supported through the Student Housing Support program.

Student Travel Support Limits

Kerala

Up to ₹2,000 or 3rd Class AC train fare (whichever is lower)

Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh

Up to ₹3,500 or 3rd Class AC train fare (whichever is lower)

Rest of India

Up to ₹5,000 or 3rd Class AC train fare (whichever is lower)
(excluding 7 North-Eastern States)

NE India, Bhutan & Nepal

Up to ₹7,500 or 3rd Class AC train fare or by other means of transport(whichever is lower)
(7 Sister States, Bhutan & Nepal)

Eligibility Criteria

1

The applicant must have a paper accepted at IPRECON 2026 and must complete full conference registration before applying for Student Travel Support.

2

The applicant must be an active IEEE Student or Graduate Student Member (GSM) with valid membership status during the conference period.

3

Priority will be given to applicants with valid IEEE IAS and/or PES Memberships in addition to their student membership.

4

Students receiving travel support from their university/Other means are not eligible for this funding. All applicants must submit a declaration confirming that they are not receiving any other travel support, in order to avoid duplication of funding.

Important Information

Support is limited and granted on a first-come, first-served basis. Reimbursement will be provided after reaching the venue and completing the registration process.

All Student Travel Support is subject to available funds and conference budget allocations. Delegates are advised to apply early to ensure consideration for financial assistance.

Application Timeline

Student Travel Support Application Opening

The application for Student Travel Support will open after the Acceptance Notification is released. Eligible participants will receive detailed application instructions via email along with their acceptance notification.
