About IPRECON

IEEE International Power and Renewable Energy Conference - India's first IEEE Club of 6 Technical Paper Conference

IPRECON

The IEEE International Power and Renewable Energy Conference legacy and foundation.

IPRECON (IEEE International Power and Renewable Energy Conference) is India's first IEEE club of 6 conferences, proudly organized by IEEE Student Branch College of Engineering Karunagappally. Since its inception, IPRECON has set a benchmark in combining innovation, sustainability, and academic excellence, offering a unified platform for students, researchers, professionals, and industry experts to collaborate on transformative solutions for the future of power and energy.

The conference has consistently maintained its commitment to excellence, providing researchers and professionals with opportunities to share cutting-edge innovations and contribute to sustainable solutions for the global energy challenges. IPRECON emphasizes the integration of power systems, renewable energy, electronics, artificial intelligence, and data science to shape a sustainable and intelligent future.

All proceedings are published in the IEEE Xplore® Digital Library, with 20% of presented papers eligible for further review and possible publication in premier IEEE IAS Periodicals, including Transactions and Magazines.

IPRECON 2026

The fourth edition continues the tradition of excellence with cutting-edge innovations and global collaboration.

4th Edition

The fourth edition of India's first and only IEEE Club of 6 Technical Paper Conference is organized by the IEEE Student Branch of the College of Engineering Karunagappally, with technical sponsorship from IEEE IAS and IEEE PES.

Scheduled from September 04–06, 2026, at College of Engineering Karunagappally, Kerala, India, the conference aims to provide a premier platform for students, researchers, and professionals to present cutting-edge innovations in the fields of power, renewable energy, and computing.

Conference Theme 2026

"Advancing Power and Renewable Energy Systems through AI, Computing, and Emerging Technologies"

The event will feature technical sessions, keynote talks, and workshops, with all accepted papers to be submitted for inclusion in the IEEE Xplore® Digital Library, subject to IEEE quality standards.
