About IPRECON 2026

The IEEE International Power and Renewable Energy Conference (IPRECON 2026) is the 4th edition of India's first and only IEEE Club of 6 Technical Paper Conference, organized by the IEEE Student Branch of the College of Engineering Karunagappally.

With technical sponsorship from IEEE IAS and IEEE PES, IPRECON 2026 serves as a premier platform for students, researchers, and professionals to share cutting-edge innovations.

All accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion in the IEEE Xplore® Digital Library

Submission Guidelines

Paper Format

  • 6-page full paper in standard IEEE format
  • Maximum 8 pages with extra payment (Rs. 1000/$25 per page)
  • No author biography at the end
  • Include references within page limit

Conference Mode

  • Hybrid mode conference
  • In-person and virtual attendance options
  • Platform details provided after registration
  • Access to all technical sessions and keynotes

Publication Benefits

  • IEEE Xplore® Digital Library inclusion
  • 20% papers eligible for IEEE IAS periodicals
  • Peer review process
  • International visibility

Registration Requirements

  • Each paper needs full registration by one author
  • IEEE membership validation required
  • Student ID verification for student category
  • Transaction ID preservation recommended
